About

Developer by craft.
Educator by choice.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Advanz101 — a Salesforce Ridge Consulting Partner — with over five years of hands-on experience. My work centers on Apex development, Lightning Web Components, and end-to-end integrations with 3rd party platforms using Apex and tools like Zapier.
