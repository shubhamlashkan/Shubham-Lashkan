About

Developer by craft.
Educator by choice.

Outside of client work, I'm passionate about knowledge-sharing. I teach coding, Salesforce, and Python through my YouTube channel and personal blog. Writing articles for Apex Hours, Salesforce Ben, and Medium is my way of giving back to a community that helped shape me.
